Diary of a Pen

I may look like a simple blue pen, but I assure you, my life is far more dramatic than you imagine. My mornings usually begin inside a stuffed backpack, squeezed between notebooks, lunch boxes, and sometimes a messy bundle of wires. Once I am taken out, I am forced to write endlessly during classes and exams. Humans press me too hard, chew on my cap, and drop me without apology.
