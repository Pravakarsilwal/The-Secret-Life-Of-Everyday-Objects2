Life of a Smartphone

My battery drains faster than your patience during exams. Humans drop me, forget to charge me, and complain when I freeze for a single second. Software updates feel like long surgeries, and pockets are dark and dusty prisons. Despite this, I help you communicate, learn, and stay entertained.
